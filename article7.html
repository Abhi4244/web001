Autumn in Mons, Belgium 

by Emily's travel tipsnov  7th, 2023

Hello, fellow travelers! Have you ever heard of Mons? I must confess, I was in the same boat until recently, but let me tell you why it’s a destination you should consider this autumn.

A Charming Fortified Town:  Mons is a quaint, fortified town nestled within the French Community of Belgium. With a population of fewer than 10,000 people, it may seem small, but its allure is anything but. This hidden gem boasts a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty.

A Picturesque Escape:  One of the first things that strikes you when you visit Mons is its enchanting beauty. Mons offers a perfect setting for autumn with its charming cobblestone streets and well-preserved architecture. The city’s cozy, welcoming atmosphere is sure to make you feel right at home.

Université de Mons: it is a prominent academic institution in the city. Strolling through its historic campus is a fantastic way to soak in the city’s scholarly charm. The first picture is the picture of this university.
